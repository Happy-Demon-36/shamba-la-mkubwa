Livestock Care Information

Chickens

Chickens need secure housing to protect from predators. Layers require calcium-rich feed for egg production, while broilers need high-protein feed for growth.

Goats

Goats need sturdy fencing as they are excellent escape artists. They require a balanced diet of forage, hay, and grains. Regular deworming is essential.

Rabbits

Rabbits need clean, well-ventilated hutches. Their diet should consist mainly of hay, supplemented with pellets and fresh vegetables. Clean water must always be available.
