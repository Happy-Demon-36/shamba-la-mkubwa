About Our Farm

Ann's Shamba is a family-owned farm dedicated to providing high-quality agricultural products. We specialize in pig farming but also offer a variety of other livestock and fresh farm produce.

Our mission is to promote sustainable farming practices while delivering healthy, nutritious products to our community.

Quality Pigs

We breed and raise healthy pigs including piglets, mother pigs, and adult pigs.

Other Livestock

Chickens, hens, goats, and rabbits all raised with care and proper nutrition.

Farm Produce

Fresh beans, maize, cabbages, and kales grown using sustainable methods.
