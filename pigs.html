Pig Farming Information

Housing

Pigs need clean, dry housing with proper ventilation. The housing should protect them from extreme weather conditions and provide enough space for movement.

Feeding

A balanced diet is crucial for pig health. We recommend commercial pig feeds supplemented with vegetables and occasional fruits. Always provide clean water.

Health Care

Regular deworming and vaccinations are essential. Watch for signs of illness like loss of appetite, coughing, or skin lesions, and consult a vet immediately.

Breeding

Pigs reach sexual maturity at 5-8 months. The gestation period is about 3 months, 3 weeks, and 3 days. Proper care during pregnancy ensures healthy litters.
